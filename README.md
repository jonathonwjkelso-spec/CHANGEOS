# ChangeOS - Change Intelligence Platform

A product of **Line Of Flight** · Created by Jonathon Kelso

## What is ChangeOS?

ChangeOS is a real-time organisational change sensing platform that analyses change initiative data through multiple predictive lenses to surface risks before they become failures.

## Features

### ChangeOS Dashboard (Demo)
- **Risk Prediction Cards** — Adoption Cliff, Attrition Risk, Technical Debt
- **Signal Clusters** — Training effectiveness, manager readiness, resistance patterns
- **Trajectory Charts** — Risk evolution over time
- **Intervention Tracking** — What should have been done and when
- **Say/Do Gap Analysis** — Delta between official narrative and ground truth

### Interactive Tools
- **Readiness Calculator** — Assess organisational readiness across 8 dimensions
- **Stakeholder Mapper** — Plot influence vs support with engagement strategies
- **Impact Assessment** — Analyse who is affected and what support they need
- **Resistance Decoder** — Diagnose what drives resistance behaviour
- **70% Myth Debunker** — The story behind the famous change statistic

### Knowledge Base
- **Framework Library** — ADKAR, Kotter, Bridges — explained and critiqued
- **Line Of Flight Methodologies** — ACCEPTANCE framework, AI-augmented change
- **Te Ao Māori & Change** — Cultural responsiveness in Aotearoa
- **Templates** — Change plans, stakeholder registers, comms templates
